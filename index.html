About Us  

About Troos

Troos Bouw is a construction company that specializes in high-quality, personalized builds of Recreational, Off-Grid, and Tiny Homes.

Our experienced team pays close attention to detail and works closely with clients to ensure their specific needs and requirements are met.

We have expertise in building homes in remote and difficult terrains and prioritize using top-quality materials for durability, longevity, sustainability and performance. Troos Bouw is dedicated to providing a unique experience that exceeds expectations.

Contact us to learn more about our services and start building your dream home.
